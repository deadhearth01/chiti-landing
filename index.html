Frequently asked questions

Who creates the content on CHITTI ?

All notes, cheat sheets, and videos are created by students who’ve recently cracked the same exams — so everything is fresh, relatable, and focused on what actually shows up.

Will CHITTI help me if I’m short on time?

Yes. CHITTI is built for smart, last-minute prep. With bite-sized cheat sheets, curated important questions, and quick revision videos, you can cover more in less time.

How do I know what to study first?

CHITTI’s structured modules and prep forecast guide you step-by-step — so you always know what’s next and never feel lost.

Is the content aligned with my university syllabus?

Absolutely. CHITTi organizes content by Program Core, Electives, and University Core — so everything matches your curriculum.

Is CHITTI free to use?

CHITTI offers focused prep for each subject at an affordable price. You pay only for the subject you need — no bundles, no hidden fees.

If I purchase a course now, can I use it next semester?

CHITTI courses are designed for the current semester only. Since syllabi and course structures can change, your access is tied to the semester you purchase in. You’ll need to repurchase if the course reappears in a future semester.
